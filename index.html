The Method

THE FRONTIER OF INTELLIGENCE IS PHYSICAL.

Enterprise tries to solve complex, multidimensional problems with flat data and cognitive overload. But the frontier of intelligence — human and artificial alike — is spatial. It is learned through physical interaction with the world.

We bring that principle to strategy. We integrate sensory experience into problem-solving, translating invisible corporate systems into something lived — so your cognitive strategy is tested against what the body already knows.
